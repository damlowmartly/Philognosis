Geometry 1

Why Learn This?

Geometry 1 introduces you to the basics of shapes, angles, and lines. These foundational concepts are essential for understanding the world around us, from architecture to art.

Explanation

In Geometry 1, you'll learn about points, lines, angles, and basic shapes like rectangles and triangles. You'll discover how to calculate perimeters, areas, and work with complementary and supplementary angles.
